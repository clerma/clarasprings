Continuing the Mission
Ministry Beyond the Summer

At Clara Springs, we believe camp is not the finish line — it is the starting point. God moves powerfully during a week at camp, but true transformation continues long after the campfire fades.

Beyond The Camp Fire exists to extend the impact of camp through ongoing encouragement, discipleship, and connection. Our desire is to see students, leaders, and churches continue growing in their faith throughout the year.

Through follow-up resources, ministry partnerships, and continued engagement, Clara Springs remains committed to helping churches and families build on what God begins during camp.
