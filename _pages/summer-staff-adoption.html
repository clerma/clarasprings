Adopt a Summer Staff Member

Each summer, Clara Springs depends on a dedicated team of young men and women who serve campers through leadership, discipleship, and daily ministry. These staff members invest deeply in students’ lives, helping create environments where lives are changed for eternity.

The Summer Staff Adoption program allows individuals, families, and churches to financially support a staff member during the summer season. Your partnership helps cover ministry expenses and enables them to focus fully on serving and discipling campers.

Most importantly, your support provides encouragement — reminding our staff that they are sent, supported, and prayed for as they serve.
