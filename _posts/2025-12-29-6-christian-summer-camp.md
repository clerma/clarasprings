In a world filled with screens, noise, and constant distraction, students are craving something deeper — connection, purpose, and truth.

That’s why Christian summer camp still matters.

At **Clara Springs**, camp isn’t just about activities or cabins. It’s about creating space for students to hear God clearly, build authentic friendships, and discover who they were created to be.

## 1) Camp removes distractions

When phones are put away and routines are interrupted, hearts open. Camp creates intentional space for worship, prayer, and reflection.

Without daily pressures and digital noise, students begin to slow down. And when they slow down, they listen.

## 2) Faith becomes personal

Church is powerful — but camp allows students to experience God for themselves.

Many lifelong callings and life-changing decisions begin around a campfire, in a worship service, or in a small-group conversation with a counselor who truly cares.

## 3) Confidence is built through challenge

From outdoor activities to leadership moments, students grow courage.

They try new things. They step outside comfort zones. They learn resilience.

## 4) Community that lasts

The friendships formed at camp are different. They’re rooted in shared spiritual experiences and genuine connection.

Clara Springs doesn’t just host events — we help cultivate moments that can shape eternity.
