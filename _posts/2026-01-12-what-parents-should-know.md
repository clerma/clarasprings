Sending your child to camp can feel like a big step.

We understand — trust matters.

Here are a few things parents often want to know before sending their child to Clara Springs.

## Safety comes first

We take safety seriously through:

- Trained staff and counselors
- Structured schedules and supervision
- Clear check-in and check-out procedures
- Age-appropriate programming

## Spiritual growth is intentional

Camp isn’t random.

Every service, small group, and activity is designed to help students grow in their relationship with Christ in a supportive, Christ-centered environment.

## First-time campers are welcome

Some campers need a gentle introduction to overnight camp — and that’s okay.

Programs like **Pre-Camp** are designed to help younger campers feel safe, confident, and excited about camp.

Learn more here: [Pre-Camp 2026](/2026-summer-camp-info/precamp/)

## Communication is clear

Parents can expect:

- Pre-camp emails
- Packing lists
- Arrival instructions
- Clear contact options if questions come up

Camp is more than a getaway.

It’s an investment in your child’s spiritual foundation.
